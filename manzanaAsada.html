Manzanas asadas

4 servings

Itensil

  • Plate or dish suitable for microwave ovens.

Ingredients

  • 4 large red apples
  • 2 tablespoons chopped raisins
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon margarine
  • 1/2 glass of cognac or sherry
  • 1 pinch of ground cinnamon

Preparation

  • Wash the apples and carefully hollow them out to avoid breaking them.
  • In a food processor, grind the almonds with the sugar and margarine.
  • Stir and add the chopped raisins.
  • Fill the apples three-quarters full with the mixture, pour in a little liquor, and sprinkle with cinnamon.
  • Place the apples separately on the plate and microwave, calculating 2 to 3 minutes per apple (or until soft) on high power.
  • Let them rest for 4-5 minutes before serving.

Cooking Time:

650-700 watts 8-9 minutes
600 watts 9-10 minutes
500 watts 10-11 minutes

Compota de peras

8 servings

Utensil

  • tempered glass or ceramic dish

Ingredients

  • 8 pears of equal size
  • 1 glass of red wine
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 cinnamon stick and lemon peels

Preparation

  • Wash and peel the pears. Place them in the glass or ceramic dish
  • Mix the wine with the water and pour it over the pears.
  • Microwave on high power (100%) for 7-8 minutes.
  • Add sugar, cinnamon, and lemon peels.
  • Return to the microwave on high power for 5-6 minutes.
  • Separate the pears from the syrup and microwave the syrup on high power for 4-5 minutes to reduce it a bit.
  • Once the syrup is cool, pour it over the pears before serving.
